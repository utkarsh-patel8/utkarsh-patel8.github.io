ABOUT US

The Developers' Society is a bunch of enthusiastic student developers eager to take on challenges, seize opportunities and utilize their skills to develop applications that will digitize several processes within the institute.



In light of the need to improve existing applications and the potential for automating various workflows on campus, the Developers' Society is an initative to bring together a group of individuals who are motivated and interested in encouraging the development culture by taking various initiatitves within the institute and developing new applications that benefit the entire KGP community.
